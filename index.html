About

Hello! I'm a Portland, OR-based vegan baker and I like decorating plant-based sweets. Find me on Happy Cow.

When I'm not baking, walking dogs (or working my day job), I'm either attending events with NW Veg and Portland Metro Veggies, keeping up with the Oregon Mycological Society, playing guitar, hiking, gardening, or spending time with friends. I love finding new plant-based eateries and spotting fungi and wildflowers on walks. Thank you for visiting my site!
