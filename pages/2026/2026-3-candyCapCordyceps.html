Candycap Cordyceps Mushroom Cookies

These cookies were made for a Renaissance Fair-themed birthday party. My partner helped paint the color gradients! We used red, yellow, and blue plant-based food coloring, along with MONÉGASQUE edible gold luster dust. I've been really enjoying using the luster dust in baking.

Cookies for another friend's celebration

Using the same dough, I made some quick and messy thumbprint cookies. Same maple-y taste, but many more nubbins. My friend's favorite color is red so the cookies used red plant-based food coloring mixed with the gold luster dust.
