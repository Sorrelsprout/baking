Fall Leaf Cookies

Fall leaf cookies made for two occasions.

Compassionate Thanksgiving 2025 Potluck

Cookies made for the event hosted by NW Veg, inspired by a fallen leaf I found while walking to get home on a mid-day fall. I thought the leaf was beautifully vibrant, with such a unique color gradient, and I had the urge to make a muse of it. To get the stems and veins the deeper red-brown color, cocoa powder from the Realm Refillery was mixed into icing along with red food coloring. The recipe is an altered version from Nora Cooks, using oat milk, and maple syrup in place of both almond extract and corn syrup.

Friend's Birthday Cookies

Warm-toned Leaves for a friend's birthday in the height of fall, made a little earlier than the cookies above. The icing is more plain and the decor is dyed with food coloring.
