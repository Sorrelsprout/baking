New Year's Eve 2025 Cupcakes

These doubled as cupcakes for a birthday party. The intent was to create cupcakes that had a magical-vintagey twist to them, hence the sun and moon graphics inspired by vintage solar symbols. Space has a vibe of ethereal endlessness to it, while a vintage aesthetic calls back to the past. Thought it would be a great way to represent a sense of "currentness" albeit subtley. Flavors made are:

  • Vanilla Custard
  • Chocolate Cherry Amaretto
  • Chocolate Peanut Butter with PB Custard
  • Pumpkin Spice Cold Brew
