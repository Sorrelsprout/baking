New Year's Eve 2025 Cupcakes

These doubled as cupcakes for a birthday party. The tops of each cupcake were cut off to give a flatter surface for decoration. Flavors made are:

  • Vanilla Custard
  • Chocolate Cherry Amaretto
  • Chocolate Peanut Butter with PB Custard
  • Pumpkin Spice Cold Brew

The Vanilla Frosting recipe from Nora Cooks was used as a base for all frostings.

The gold lettering was done by first making and piping the Easy Icing from Nora Cooks's Sugar Cookie Recipe onto the iced cupcake surface. Then mixing the water-insoluble MONÃ‰GASQUE edible gold luster dust with water quickly to form a pasty paintable solution. The thickened paste was painted onto the iced letters/designs quickly, tiny bit by tiny bit, to make the designs shine. Water was used as a means to paint the powder onto the designs because the drying time was a lot faster and cleanup was easier. The powder also wasn't mixed with the icing itself because I felt like by painting on the powder, it would save on the amount of powder used, and give the designs themselves more oomph.

Inspiration

The intent was to create cupcakes that had a magical-vintagey twist to them, hence the sun and moon graphics inspired by vintage solar symbols. Space has a vibe of ethereal endlessness to it, while a vintage aesthetic calls back to the past. Thought it would be a great way to represent a sense of "currentness" albeit subtley. I also wanted a darker color scheme for a cake base to contrast well with the gold that I knew I'd be using for lettering.

Chocolate Cherry Amaretto

This used an adjusted version of the Black Forest Cupcakes recipe from Addicted to Dates. No Swiss Merengue Buttercream was used, and instead the frosting used an adjusted version of the vanilla frosting recipe referenced above.

Vanilla Custard

  • This used an adjusted Vanilla Cupcakes recipe from Loving it Vegan. I didn't add the strawberries due to wanting a plainer flavor, and one of my friends having a strawberry allergy. The constraints for this project were good and felt manageable to work with.
  • The Custard recipe from Nora Cooks was used to make the filling for these.

Chocolate Peanut Butter, Pumpkin Spice Cold Brew

Pumpkin Spice Cold Brew is terracotta with gold. It's an adapted version of the Loving it Vegan Vanilla cupcake recipe referenced above.

Chocolate Peanut Butter is dark brown with gold. It's based off of this Chocolate Cupcake recipe from Loving It Vegan.
